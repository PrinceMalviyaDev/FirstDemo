Chocolate Cake

  • 1 cup of Flour
  • 1/2 cup of Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup of Cocoa Powder

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all the dry ingredients together.
  3. Add the eggs and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy your delicious chocolate cake!
